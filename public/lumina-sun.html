02 — The Challenge

Solar data is complex.
Decisions shouldn't be.

Operators juggle disconnected portals, raw inverter logs and spreadsheets — losing yield and money to problems they can't see until it's too late.

Fragmented monitoring

Separate apps for inverters, batteries and meters with no single source of truth.

Difficult performance tracking

No clear baseline of expected vs actual output makes underperformance invisible.

Hidden energy losses

Soiling, shading and string faults quietly erode generation for weeks unnoticed.

Lack of financial visibility

Owners can't connect kilowatt-hours to dollars, ROI or payback timelines.

Reactive maintenance

Faults are found after revenue is lost, not before — truck rolls cost more.

No predictive context

Weather and irradiance forecasts are ignored, so yield planning is guesswork.
